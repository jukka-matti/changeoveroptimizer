## Appendix: Data Collection Tips

### How to Measure Changeover Time

**Start Point:** Last good piece of previous product
**End Point:** First good piece of new product

**Include:**
- Setup and adjustment
- Cleaning
- Material changes
- First-piece inspection
- Waiting for approval

**Measure multiple times** (at least 3) to understand variability.

### Where to Find Data

- Production logs
- MES/ERP system
- Shift reports
- Direct observation
- Operator interviews

### Common Mistakes

- Using estimated times instead of actual
- Forgetting waiting and walking time
- Only measuring "good" changeovers
- Not recording who did the changeover
