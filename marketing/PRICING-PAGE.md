## FAQ SECTION

### Headline

## Frequently Asked Questions

---

**Is there a free trial?**

Yes! 14 days with full features. No credit card required. Just download and start using.

---

**What happens after the trial ends?**

The software will prompt you to purchase. Your data is saved, so you can pick up right where you left off after purchasing.

---

**Can I switch from monthly to annual?**

Yes, anytime. Contact support and we'll prorate your remaining monthly subscription toward an annual plan.

---

**What payment methods do you accept?**

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal. Payments are processed securely by Paddle.

---

**Do you handle EU VAT?**

Yes. Paddle handles all EU VAT compliance. You'll see the correct VAT rate for your country at checkout. Business customers can enter their VAT ID for reverse charge.

---

**Can I get an invoice?**

Yes. All purchases include a proper invoice sent to your email. You can also access invoices in your account.

---

**What if I'm not satisfied?**

For software: Cancel within 14 days of purchase for a full refund.
For course: 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked.

---

**Do you offer team/volume pricing?**

For teams of 5+, contact us for volume discounts. We offer 20% off for 5-9 licenses and 30% off for 10+.

---

**Can I use this for multiple production lines?**

Yes! One license covers one computer. You can manage multiple lines within the software. For multiple users/computers, each needs a license.

---

**Is my data secure?**

Your data stays on your computer. We don't have access to your production data. The software works offline after activation.

---

**What platforms are supported?**

- Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
- macOS 10.15+ (Catalina and newer)

---

**How do updates work?**

Updates are automatic and free for the duration of your subscription. You'll be notified when updates are available.

---

**Can I export my data?**

Yes. Export schedules to Excel, print SMED studies and standard work to PDF. Your data is always accessible.

---

**Do I need the course to use the software?**

No. The software is fully functional without the course. The course teaches the methodology behind the software and is recommended for best results, but not required.

---

**Is the course self-paced?**

Yes. Take as long as you need. You have lifetime access to the course materials.

---

**Do you offer consulting services?**

Our parent company RDMAIC Oy offers Lean Six Sigma consulting. Contact us for on-site implementation support.
